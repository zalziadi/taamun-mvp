# برومبت Stitch — تصميم صفحة المدينة التفاعلية

```
IDEA: Interactive "City of Meaning" (مدينة المعنى) page for a Quranic contemplation web app called Taamun (تمعّن). This is a spiritual progress visualization — a dark-themed city map that progressively "lights up" as the user completes 28 days of daily contemplation. The city has 9 life domains arranged in a circle around a central core.

THEME: Dark spiritual — deep charcoal (#15130f) background with warm gold (#e6d4a4) accents. The mood is contemplative, sacred, and alive. Think of a city at night that gradually illuminates — from complete darkness to full golden glow. The aesthetic is Arabic calligraphic minimalism with soft radial light effects. Fonts: Amiri for Arabic headlines, clean sans-serif for labels. Direction: RTL (Arabic).

CONTENT:

SCREEN 1 — City Map (0 days completed / Dark State):
- Header: "CITY OF MEANING" subtitle + "مدينة المعنى" main title
- Description: "القرآن ليس مجرد نص، بل نظام تحويل داخلي"
- Central SVG map showing 9 domain circles around a center node:
  - Center: "مدينة المعنى — وعي · إدراك · أثر"
  - 9 domains (all DARK/dimmed): الهوية، العلاقات، النمو، البناء، الجمال، الأسرة، المراجعة، المال، العطاء
  - Connecting lines from center to each domain (very faint)
  - Each domain circle is dim (#2b2824) with barely visible text
- Right sidebar: Selected domain panel showing question + 3 awareness state buttons (ظل / هدية / أفضل احتمال)
- Bottom: Progress indicator showing "0/28 يوم" with empty progress ring

SCREEN 2 — City Map (7 days completed / Partial Glow):
- Same layout but NOW:
  - 2-3 domain circles are partially lit (soft golden glow, brighter stroke)
  - The connecting lines to lit domains have a warm golden pulse
  - Center node has a faint inner glow starting to appear
  - A subtle radial gradient emanates from lit nodes
  - Progress ring shows "7/28" with partial fill
  - Small milestone badge appears: "أتممت الأسبوع الأول ✦"

SCREEN 3 — City Map (28 days completed / Full Illumination):
- The entire city is NOW alive with golden light:
  - All 9 domain circles are fully illuminated with golden glow and soft halos
  - Center node is brightest — radiating light outward
  - Connecting lines are golden with animated pulse effect
  - Background has subtle golden particles/stars
  - The city feels ALIVE and warm — like a living spiritual map
  - Progress ring is complete: "28/28 يوم — اكتملت المدينة"
  - Achievement overlay: "مدينتك أصبحت مضيئة" with a calligraphic design

DESIGN DETAILS:
- Domain glow levels should correlate to the number of days in each stage:
  - 0 days = completely dark
  - 1-3 days = faint ember glow
  - 4-7 days = soft warm light
  - 8-14 days = steady golden glow
  - 15-21 days = bright with subtle halo
  - 22-28 days = full radiant illumination with particles
- The visual progression should feel organic, not mechanical
- Use radial gradients and soft shadows, not hard edges
- The feeling should be: "my inner city is coming alive"
```
