## Milestone philosophy

Each milestone = one shippable slice of value. Between milestones, the codebase is always deployable.

- **v1.0:** Make it work (core loop)
- **v1.1:** Make it sticky (growth & retention — email, push, AI cycles, book, WhatsApp infra)
- **v1.2:** Close the loop (retention — Day 28 → Year 1 continuity: cycle-2 UX, badges, year-in-review, renewals, referrals, analytics)
- **v1.3+:** Make it personal (depth — ML themes, long-term memory, voice journaling, a11y) — deferred from original v1.2 backlog
- **v2.0:** Make it a platform (multi-user, creator-driven)
