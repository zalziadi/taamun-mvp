# Requirements — v1.6 (archived)

Snapshot of v1.6 requirements as shipped 2026-04-20.

---

## Mission

Make the invisible work visible: earned invite credits, creator follower
graph, and moderation queue signal to the founder.

---

## Must-have ✅

- ✅ `/account`: surface earned invite days (total + per-redemption log available via API)
- ✅ Creator follow system: DB + API + button on `/journey/[slug]`
- ✅ New-journey push fan-out to followers on draft→published transition
- ✅ Daily moderation summary email to founder (silent when empty or unconfigured)

---

## Open questions — resolved

1. **Credits UI:** ✅ inline in InviteShare widget
2. **Follow default:** ✅ off, explicit tap
3. **Mod digest:** ✅ daily 08:00 UTC, email-only, silent on empty queue
4. **Follower count visibility:** ✅ creator-only (not public)
