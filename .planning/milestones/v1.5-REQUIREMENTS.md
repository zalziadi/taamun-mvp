# Requirements — v1.5 (archived)

Snapshot of v1.5 requirements as shipped 2026-04-20.

---

## Mission

Close the loop around the community + creator surfaces opened in v1.4:
trust (moderation), signal (analytics), re-engagement (reply push),
discoverability (in-app activations).

---

## Requirements

### Must-have ✅

- ✅ Moderation dashboard covering threads, thread_replies, creator_journeys, shared_insights
- ✅ Thread reply push notification to the original author
- ✅ Creator analytics per journey (subscribers, completion, per-day)
- ✅ `/discover` and `/creator` entry points (nav + /account)

### Should-have ✅

- ✅ "ناقش هذه الآية" button on day pages linking to `/threads?anchor_type=verse&anchor_value=…`
- ⏭ `rewarded_at` visibility in `/account` — deferred to v1.6 (needs UI design)

### Nice-to-have — deferred

- ⏭ Creator-follow notifications
- ⏭ Multi-language creator journey tagging

---

## Open questions — resolved

1. **Moderation actions:** ✅ full — approve/remove/keep
2. **Reply push default:** ✅ ON (silent no-op when unconfigured)
3. **Analytics visibility:** ✅ creator-only
4. **Nav gating:** ✅ discover public, creator VIP-only via `/account`
