# Requirements — v2.0 (archived)

Snapshot of v2.0 requirements as shipped 2026-04-20.

---

## Mission

Expat Muslim discovery through an English marketing bridge. Arabic stays
canonical; English landing + FAQ point back to the Arabic app.

---

## Must-have ✅

- ✅ English landing at `/en`
- ✅ English FAQ at `/en/faq` with FAQPage schema
- ✅ hreflang alternates wiring both directions
- ✅ Language switcher in global nav

---

## Out of scope (deferred)

- Arabic → English translation of the authed app (program, guide, account)
- English-only creator journeys
- Runtime direction toggle

---

## Open questions — resolved

1. **Scope:** ✅ marketing only (`/en`, `/en/faq`)
2. **CTAs:** ✅ all point back to the Arabic app with disclosure
3. **Switcher placement:** ✅ AppChrome header, next to NavAuth
4. **Canonical URL strategy:** ✅ self-canonical per language + hreflang cross-reference
