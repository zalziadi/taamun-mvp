## Decisions Made

- **32-char Crockford alphabet, not 30-char.** The user prompt's `<critical_rules>` suggested excluding `I/L/O/0/1` (30 chars). The canonical PLAN.md, however, specifies the 32-char Crockford variant excluding `I/L/O/U` with the regex `/^FRIEND-[0-9A-HJKMNP-TV-Z]{6}$/`, and declares that regex an exported contract imported by Plans 10.03 and 10.04. Followed the PLAN.md because:
  1. It is the explicit contract downstream plans bind to.
  2. 32 = 2^5 is a perfect divisor of 256, yielding bias-free `byte % 32` extraction. 30 would require rejection sampling to stay unbiased.
  3. Crockford standard excludes `I/L/O/U` — `0`/`1` are kept because their digit semantics disambiguate them from letters at a glance.
- **Service-role admin client required for uniqueness check** — noted in the JSDoc so 10.03 does not mistakenly pass the anon/SSR client (which would miss rows under RLS).
- **No REFACTOR commit** — the implementation is already the minimal path; additional abstraction would violate CLAUDE.md rule 8 ("ممنوع التبسيط/إعادة الهيكلة التخمينية").
